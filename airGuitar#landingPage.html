Air Guitar. Anywhere. Any time.


With your iPhone and Apple Watch, you can experience the wonders of Air Guitar. Strum with your watch hand and show your chord skills on your phone. You'll be amazed by how smooth the feel and sound experience is!

iPhone + Apple Watch

When we first thought of this idea, it seemed almost too obvious. Utilizing Apple Watch's accelerometer and the interactivity of iPhone touchscreen capabilities, we reimagined the air guitar concept to produce real sound with real motions. Try it for yourself!

It's beautiful

The app is a sleek makeover of your traditional guitar concept. A modern and bold design of the strings and frets come with realistic vibrational feedback - designed to respond based on the strength of the strum and position of your fingers.

From the moment you open the app to the final strum of your tune, you'll see just how much design thought went into creating this beautiful interface.

Extremely Technical

From filtering noisy accelerometer data to designing our own synthesizer with a delayed strum effect proportional to velocity of your strum motion, our app is rich with technical intricacy. The depth and complexity of the app is seamless to provide a smooth user experience.
